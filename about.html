What is MOVXIO?

MOVXIO is a free, ad-supported streaming platform built for movie lovers who believe great films should be accessible to everyone. No subscriptions. No sign-ups. No credit cards. Just press play.

We host a curated library of films spanning action, drama, comedy, thriller, horror, sci-fi, romance, documentary, and more — including local language content in Sinhala, Tamil, Hindi, and Korean.

Our Mission

To make quality entertainment freely accessible to everyone, everywhere — without barriers, without paywalls, and without compromising the viewing experience.

We believe the best stories deserve the widest audience. MOVXIO exists to bridge the gap between great filmmakers and the people who want to watch their work — regardless of their location or financial situation.

How We Work

MOVXIO is supported entirely by advertising. When you watch a film on MOVXIO, you may see ads displayed on the page. This revenue allows us to license content, maintain our infrastructure, and keep the platform free for all users.

We use HLS (HTTP Live Streaming) technology to deliver high-quality adaptive video — meaning the player automatically adjusts quality based on your connection speed for a smooth, buffer-free experience.

Content Library

Our library includes movies and TV series across all major genres. All content on MOVXIO is either licensed, in the public domain, or hosted with the permission of the rights holders. We take copyright seriously — if you believe content has been posted without authorization, please see our DMCA policy.

Technology

MOVXIO is built with modern web technologies — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the frontend, powered by Supabase for our database and Cloudflare for global content delivery. Our video infrastructure uses Cloudflare R2 for storage and HLS for adaptive streaming.

Based In

MOVXIO operates globally with a focus on providing accessible entertainment to audiences in South Asia and beyond.
